We are looking for a highly motivated postdoctoral researcher to join our lab at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign. The position focuses on developing machine learning methods to better understand complex biological regulation from genomic and spatial data.

Project Overview

The project studies how gene regulation is encoded across the genome and how it varies across tissues, cell types, and disease contexts. This project will focus on building deep learning models and exploring emerging agentic AI methods to decode regulatory structures, particularly from spatial and single-cell genomics data. The work spans data integration, model development, and biological interpretation, with opportunities to collaborate across computational and experimental teams.

The project is expected to start around Aug. 2025
